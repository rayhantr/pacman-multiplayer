How to play

Use the arrow keys or the on-screen pad to move.

Pac-Men: collect all pellets to win.

Ghosts: catch a Pac-Man to turn it into a ghost. Win once every Pac-Man has been caught.

Power-ups (a new boost every ~15s, vanishes after 20s if uncollected)

Each item only works for its own team — the other side walks over it.

Speed boost (10 seconds)
Invincibility — eat ghosts (5 seconds)
Pellet multiplier (doubles score, 10 seconds)
Pellet magnet — vacuum nearby pellets (6 seconds)
Phase — pass through walls (3 seconds)
Freeze — stun the other team (2.5 seconds)
